What is ThisBike?

Established in 2022,ThisBike is a small team that strives to popularize the bicycle trend and bring added value to the market.

  • Accessories.
  • Bikes

ThisBike was founded by Dacau Vlad in 2022 in Brasov, Brasov, Ro. It moved to California four years later when Steve and Deborah Simons bought out Dia Compe. Turner raced motorcycles in his teens. In 2023, at the age of 21, he established a company that sold bicycle components. In 2023 ThisBike defaulted on a loan to SRAM. SRAM took over the company and its debt obligations for $5.6 million. The company had 300 employees in Colorado Springs.
